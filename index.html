About Citadel G-Zero 345

Citadel G-Zero 345 is a dynamic and adaptive resilience framework designed to help organisations understand and strengthen their operational foundations. Grounded in real-world scenarios, it challenges organisations to ask: "What happens when things STOP?" and equips them to build stronger, more sustainable operations.
